# 🎓 MoodMap-Student-Productivity-Tracker - Track Your Mood, Sleep, and Study Habits Easily

## 🚀 Getting Started
Welcome to MoodMap-Student-Productivity-Tracker! This is a simple tool that helps students keep track of their mood, sleep, and study hours. By analyzing this data, you can discover your productivity patterns and improve your study habits.

## 🎯 Features
MoodMap-Student-Productivity-Tracker offers several key features:

- **Mood Tracking**: Easily log your mood at different times of the day.
- **Sleep Log**: Keep track of your sleep hours and patterns.
- **Study Hour Tracking**: Record the hours you spend studying each day.
- **Productivity Analysis**: View your productivity patterns based on your recorded data.

These features will allow you to reflect on your habits and adjustments in your studies.

## 📝 How to Use
1. **Launch the Application**: After downloading, double-click the MoodMap icon to open the tool.
2. **Enter Data**:
   - Choose the current date and time.
   - Select your mood from a simple dropdown menu.
   - Input your sleep hours and study hours.
3. **View Reports**:
   - Access your mood and productivity reports to analyze trends over time.

By regularly updating your entries, you can gain insights into how your moods and study habits affect each other.
